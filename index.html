FAQs

What is ghostwriting, and how does it work?

Ghostwriting is when a professional writer creates content—such as books, articles, or blog posts—on behalf of someone else. The credited author provides the ideas, and the ghostwriter turns them into written works.

Can I retain full rights to a ghostwritten book?

Yes, as the client, you retain full rights to the ghostwritten content. Once the project is completed, you can publish, distribute, and claim the work as your own.

How do you help with Etsy store creation?

I offer complete Etsy store setup services, including product listings, SEO optimization, and store branding. This helps ensure your shop is ready to attract customers and generate sales.

What are the benefits of optimizing product listings on Etsy?

Optimizing product listings improves your store’s visibility on Etsy, increasing the chances of your products being found by potential buyers. This includes keyword research, compelling descriptions, and high-quality images.

What services do you provide for Shopify store setup?

I offer a full range of Shopify services, including store creation, theme customization, product uploads, and integrating essential apps for payment and marketing. This ensures your Shopify store is functional and ready for sales.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify?

Yes, I can help migrate your store from platforms like WooCommerce, Magento, or Etsy to Shopify. This includes transferring products, customer data, and ensuring a seamless transition.

What is Framer, and how does it improve web design?

Framer is a design tool used for building fully interactive prototypes and websites. It allows for smooth transitions, animations, and modern user experiences, making your website more dynamic and engaging.

Can you help design a website using Framer?

Absolutely! I provide design services using Framer to create responsive, interactive websites that are tailored to your brand and business needs. From initial concept to a fully functional site, I can assist every step of the way.
